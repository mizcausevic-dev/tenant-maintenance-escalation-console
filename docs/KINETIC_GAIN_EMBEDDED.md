# Kinetic Gain Embedded

`tenant-maintenance-escalation-console` exists to prove that Kinetic Gain OS can extend into PropTech and real-estate operations without losing the operator-surface framing.

## Embedded tie-back

- **Service safety**: emergency, recurring, and compliance-related tickets can be blocked when the maintenance packet drifts from dispatch or resident reality.
- **Operator visibility**: property operations, maintenance, finance, and resident-support teams see the same escalation state.
- **Commercial path**: this can ladder into hosted property-ops templates, vendor-governance reviews, and embedded service-operations consulting.

## Why this matters

Many property teams treat maintenance escalation as a pile of tickets, calls, invoices, and portal notes. This repo makes the governance layer visible: which tickets are safe, where escalation evidence is stale, and why the next resident update should or should not leave the dock.
